Cardiology

Our cardiology department provides comprehensive cardiac care, including diagnostic tests, interventional procedures, and management of heart conditions to promote cardiovascular health.

With a patient-centered approach, cutting-edge technology, and a commitment to excellence, our Cardiology Department is dedicated to promoting cardiovascular health and ensuring the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists offer advanced treatments for bone and joint disorders, providing surgical and non-surgical interventions, sports medicine, and rehabilitation services for optimal musculoskeletal health.

By combining advanced treatments, personalized care plans, and a multidisciplinary approach, our Orthopedics Department strives to help you regain mobility, reduce pain, and enhance your overall musculoskeletal health.

Oncology

Our oncology department specializes in the diagnosis, treatment, and management of various types of cancers, offering state-of-the-art therapies, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and targeted treatments.

Does Hope Health accept insurance plans?

Yes, we accept a wide range of insurance plans. We recommend contacting our billing department or checking our website for a list of accepted insurance providers to ensure coverage.

  • How can I make an appointment at Hope Health?

    Making an appointment is easy. You can book online through our website, call our appointment helpline, or visit our hospital in person to schedule an appointment with the respective department.

  • What should I bring for my appointment?

    Please bring your identification documents, insurance card, any relevant medical records or test results, and a list of current medications you are taking. It is also helpful to arrive a few minutes early to complete any necessary paperwork.

  • Does Hope Health have an emergency department?

    Yes, we have a dedicated emergency department that operates 24/7. Our emergency department is staffed by experienced healthcare professionals who are ready to provide immediate medical care for critical and urgent cases.

  • Does Hope Health offer financial assistance or payment plans?

    We understand that medical expenses can be a concern. We have financial assistance programs available for eligible patients. Please reach out to our billing department for more information regarding payment options and assistance programs.

  • Does Hope Health have specialized clinics or programs?

    Yes, we have specialized clinics and programs for various medical conditions and treatments. These may include specialized clinics for diabetes management, weight management, women's health, and more. Please inquire with our staff or visit our website for more details.
